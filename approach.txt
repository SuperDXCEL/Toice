TOICE

App to protect vulnerable citizens from scam calls
using AI-generated voices and social engineering.

First step:

Incoming call risk scoring. Known contacts pass through.
Unknown numbers trigger full analysis pipeline.

Second step:

Analyze the caller's voice for synthetic speech detection.
(RawNetLite)

Third step:

Real-time speech to text and LLM analysis to detect
social engineering patterns and scam language.
(Whisper + LLM)

Fourth step:

Compare caller's voice against stored family voiceprints,
built progressively from previous calls.
(ECAPA-TDNN)

Fifth step:

Guardian notified in real time with risk level,
live transcription, and one-tap hang up option.

All processing runs on-device or private server.
Zero data sent to third parties. Full GDPR compliance.

Target: families with elderly or vulnerable members.
"Hiya protects businesses. Toice protects families."
